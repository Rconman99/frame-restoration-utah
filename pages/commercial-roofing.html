Commercial Roofing

Flat Roofing Systems — TPO, EPDM & Modified Bitumen

Utah's commercial properties need roofing systems engineered for extreme UV exposure, heavy snow loads, and dramatic temperature swings. Frame Roofing Utah installs and services TPO, EPDM, and modified bitumen systems with meticulous attention to seams, flashing, and penetration details — the failure points that cheaper contractors skip. From new construction to full tear-off replacements, we deliver industrial-grade flat roofing that performs for decades.

Metal Roofing — Built to Last

Standing seam and corrugated metal roofing systems for warehouses, industrial facilities, and commercial buildings that demand maximum lifespan with minimal maintenance. Frame Roofing Utah installs metal roofing with precision-engineered panel alignment, concealed fasteners, and proper thermal movement allowances — ensuring a watertight system that handles Utah's toughest conditions year after year.

Multifamily & HOA — Large-Scale, Minimal Disruption

Apartment complexes, condominiums, and HOA communities across the Wasatch Front trust Frame Roofing Utah for large-scale re-roofing projects. We coordinate phased installations that minimize disruption to residents, manage HOA approval processes, and deliver consistent quality across every building. One crew, one standard, one point of contact — from first walkthrough to final inspection.

Roof Maintenance Programs — Protect Your Investment

A proactive maintenance program extends your commercial roof's service life by years and catches small problems before they become expensive emergencies. Frame Roofing Utah offers scheduled inspection and maintenance programs tailored to your roofing system — including drain clearing, seam checks, flashing inspections, and detailed condition reports so you always know the state of your roof.
