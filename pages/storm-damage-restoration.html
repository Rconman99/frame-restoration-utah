Hail Damage — Expert Assessment & Full-Scope Repair

Utah's Wasatch Front averages multiple significant hailstorms per year, with the most active season running from May through September. Frame Roofing Utah identifies all hail damage — including the damage you cannot see from the ground — with a thorough, no-pressure inspection. We document every impact point, photograph all findings, and ensure your insurance claim covers the full repair cost. Our crews use premium materials and meticulous installation techniques so your restored roof looks and performs better than before the storm.

Common hail damage signs include dented or bruised shingles, cracked flashing, damaged gutters and downspouts, and granule loss that exposes the asphalt mat underneath. Even if your roof looks fine from ground level, hail impacts at shingle level can dramatically reduce your roof's remaining lifespan. A free inspection from Frame Roofing Utah catches what the eye misses.

Wind Damage — Fast Response, Permanent Solutions

High winds channeling through Utah's canyons can rip shingles, lift flashing, and expose your roof deck to the elements. Frame Roofing Utah responds fast with emergency tarping to prevent further water intrusion, then delivers permanent repairs built to industrial-grade standards. No temporary patches — we restore your roof with the same uncompromising quality we bring to every project.

Snow & Ice Damage — Built for Utah's Toughest Conditions

Heavy snow loads, ice dams, and relentless freeze-thaw cycles are part of life along the Wasatch Front and in Utah's mountain communities. Frame Roofing Utah repairs snow and ice damage with proper ventilation upgrades, ice and water shield installation, and structural reinforcement — addressing the root cause so the problem does not come back next winter. For mountain communities like Heber City and Park City, we engineer every repair for the specific conditions at elevation.

Insurance Claim Navigation — A Frictionless Process

Filing an insurance claim should not add stress to an already stressful situation. Frame Roofing Utah works directly with your adjuster, provides detailed damage documentation with photographic evidence, and advocates on your behalf to ensure you receive the full payout your policy allows. From first call to final walkthrough, we handle every detail so you can focus on what matters.

Our insurance process includes a free damage assessment, comprehensive photo documentation of all impact points, a written scope-of-work estimate that matches insurer formatting, direct adjuster coordination, and supplement filing when initial estimates fall short. We have helped hundreds of Utah homeowners recover the full value of their claims when other contractors accepted the first offer.
