Storm Damage Restoration

Hail Damage — Expert Assessment & Full-Scope Repair

Utah's Wasatch Front averages multiple significant hailstorms per year. Frame Restoration identifies all hail damage — including the damage you can't see from the ground — with a thorough, no-pressure inspection. We document every impact point, photograph all findings, and ensure your insurance claim covers the full repair cost. Our crews use premium materials and meticulous installation techniques so your restored roof looks and performs better than before the storm.

Wind Damage — Fast Response, Permanent Solutions

High winds channeling through Utah's canyons can rip shingles, lift flashing, and expose your roof deck to the elements. Frame Restoration responds fast with emergency tarping to prevent further damage, then delivers permanent repairs built to industrial-grade standards. No temporary patches — we restore your roof with the same uncompromising quality we bring to every project.

Snow & Ice Damage — Built for Utah's Toughest Conditions

Heavy snow loads, ice dams, and relentless freeze-thaw cycles are part of life along the Wasatch Front and in Utah's mountain communities. Frame Restoration repairs snow and ice damage with proper ventilation upgrades, ice and water shield installation, and structural reinforcement — addressing the root cause so the problem doesn't come back next winter.

Insurance Claim Navigation — A Frictionless Process

Filing an insurance claim shouldn't add stress to an already stressful situation. Frame Restoration works directly with your adjuster, provides detailed damage documentation with photographic evidence, and advocates on your behalf to ensure you receive the full payout you're entitled to. From first call to final walkthrough, we handle every detail so you can focus on what matters.
