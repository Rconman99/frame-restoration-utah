Restoration Services

Water Damage Restoration

A burst pipe at 2 a.m. or a slow appliance leak behind the wall — water damage demands precision and speed. Frame Restoration deploys IICRC-certified technicians within hours, using commercial-grade extraction equipment, thermal imaging to map hidden moisture, and industrial drying systems that restore your property to pre-loss condition. Every step is documented with moisture readings and photo evidence so nothing is left to guesswork — and your insurance claim is airtight from the start.

Fire & Smoke Damage Restoration

The visible damage from a fire is only the beginning. Smoke and soot infiltrate HVAC ductwork, insulation, wall cavities, and soft materials long after the flames are out — causing ongoing health risks and structural deterioration if left untreated. Frame Restoration provides comprehensive fire restoration: safe debris removal, deep smoke and soot remediation, advanced odor elimination, and full structural rebuild to exacting standards. We treat every fire-damaged property with the care and thoroughness your home deserves.

Flood Damage Mitigation

Utah's spring snowmelt surges and intense summer thunderstorms create acute flood risk along the Wasatch Front — particularly in canyon-adjacent communities like Heber, Midway, and Park City where runoff patterns are unpredictable. Frame Restoration delivers emergency flood response with rapid water extraction, antimicrobial treatment, professional mold prevention, and complete property restoration. We coordinate directly with your insurance carrier from day one, handling documentation and billing so you can focus on your family.

Insurance Claim Assistance

Filing a restoration claim shouldn't add stress to an already difficult situation. Frame Restoration works shoulder-to-shoulder with your insurance adjuster, providing detailed damage documentation complete with moisture readings, thermal imaging reports, and comprehensive photo evidence. We ensure your claim reflects the full scope of necessary repairs — no gaps, no surprises. Our transparent, itemized estimates give you and your adjuster complete confidence in every line item.
