Solar Installation

Residential Solar Systems

Most Utah homes are ideal for solar. Frame Restoration designs and installs residential solar arrays sized for your household's energy use, roof orientation, and shading conditions. Because we're roofers first, your roof warranty stays intact and your panels are mounted properly from day one.

Roof-Integrated Solar

Replacing your roof? It's the perfect time to go solar. Frame Restoration bundles roof replacement with solar installation so you get a brand-new roof and solar system in a single project — saving time, money, and avoiding future re-mounting costs.

Net Metering & Utility Setup

Utah's net metering program through Rocky Mountain Power lets you earn credits for excess energy your panels produce. Frame Restoration handles all permitting, utility interconnection paperwork, and system commissioning so you start saving from day one.

Battery Backup & Energy Storage

Pair your solar array with battery storage for energy independence and backup power during outages. Frame Restoration installs leading battery systems that integrate seamlessly with your solar panels and home electrical system.
