Solar Installation

Residential Solar Systems

With 225+ days of sunshine per year, most Utah homes are exceptionally well-suited for solar. Frame Restoration designs and installs residential solar arrays precisely sized for your household's energy consumption, roof pitch, orientation, and shading conditions. Because we are roofers first, every mounting point is engineered to protect your roof's integrity — your warranty stays intact and your panels are secured to the highest structural standards from day one.

Roof-Integrated Solar

Replacing your roof is the ideal moment to go solar. Frame Restoration bundles premium roof replacement with solar installation into a single, coordinated project — eliminating the need for future panel removal, re-mounting, and the warranty complications that come with them. One crew, one timeline, one point of accountability. You get a brand-new roof and a high-performance solar system engineered together from the start.

Net Metering & Utility Setup

Utah's net metering program through Rocky Mountain Power allows you to earn credits for every kilowatt-hour of excess energy your panels produce. Frame Restoration manages the entire process end to end — from permit applications and engineering reviews to utility interconnection paperwork and final system commissioning. We handle the complexity so you start generating savings immediately.

Battery Backup & Energy Storage

Pair your solar array with battery storage for true energy independence and reliable backup power during grid outages. Frame Restoration installs industry-leading battery systems that integrate seamlessly with your solar panels and home electrical infrastructure — giving you control over your energy use around the clock, whether the grid is up or down.
