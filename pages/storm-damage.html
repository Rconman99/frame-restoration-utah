Hail Damage Repair

Utah sits in the heart of the Wasatch Front hail belt, where summer storms routinely produce golf-ball-sized hail that destroys shingles, dents gutters, and cracks ridge caps. Frame Roofing Utah specializes in identifying hail damage that untrained eyes miss — the subtle bruising and granule loss that insurance adjusters look for during inspections.

After a hailstorm, time matters. Most insurance policies require you to file a claim within one year of the damage date, and waiting too long can mean a denied claim. We provide free hail damage assessments and handle the entire insurance process so you get the full coverage you deserve.

Wind & Snow Damage

Canyon winds along the Wasatch Front can gust above 100 mph, tearing shingles clean off, lifting flashing, and exposing your roof deck to moisture. Heavy snow loads — especially in areas like Park City, Heber City, and the benches above Salt Lake — create ice dams that force water underneath shingles and into your attic.

Whether it is wind-lifted shingles, crushed vents from snow weight, or ice dam leaks dripping through your ceiling, our crews know exactly how to assess and repair these Utah-specific problems quickly and permanently.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do immediately after a hailstorm in Utah?

Document damage with ground-level photos, avoid climbing on the roof, and call Frame Roofing Utah at 435-302-4422 for a free storm-damage inspection within 24 hours. We meet your insurance adjuster on-site to make sure every piece of damage is captured.

Does insurance cover hail damage to my roof?

Most Utah homeowner policies cover sudden hail and wind damage. Frame Roofing Utah documents damage with photos and drone imagery and submits supplements when initial adjuster scopes miss items, so your claim reflects the full scope of work.

How long do I have to file a storm-damage claim in Utah?

Typical Utah policies allow 45 to 60 days from the date of loss. Don't wait — damage worsens with every freeze-thaw cycle, and late filings get denied. Call 435-302-4422 to start the inspection process.

Is storm-damage inspection free?

Yes. Every Frame Roofing Utah storm-damage inspection is 100% free and no-obligation across the Wasatch Front. We'll give you an honest assessment of whether you have a claim worth filing before you call your insurance company.
