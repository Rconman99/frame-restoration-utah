Storm Damage

Hail Damage Repair

Utah sits in the heart of the Wasatch Front hail belt, where summer storms routinely produce golf-ball-sized hail that destroys shingles, dents gutters, and cracks ridge caps. Frame Roofing Utah specializes in identifying hail damage that untrained eyes miss — the subtle bruising and granule loss that insurance adjusters look for during inspections.

After a hailstorm, time matters. Most insurance policies require you to file a claim within one year of the damage date, and waiting too long can mean a denied claim. We provide free hail damage assessments and handle the entire insurance process so you get the full coverage you deserve.

Wind & Snow Damage

Canyon winds along the Wasatch Front can gust above 100 mph, tearing shingles clean off, lifting flashing, and exposing your roof deck to moisture. Heavy snow loads — especially in areas like Park City, Heber City, and the benches above Salt Lake — create ice dams that force water underneath shingles and into your attic.

Whether it is wind-lifted shingles, crushed vents from snow weight, or ice dam leaks dripping through your ceiling, our crews know exactly how to assess and repair these Utah-specific problems quickly and permanently.

Emergency Storm Response

Storms do not wait for business hours, and neither do we. Frame Roofing Utah offers 24/7 emergency storm response across the entire Wasatch Front. When a storm tears through your neighborhood, our team can be on-site the same day to tarp exposed areas, prevent further water intrusion, and start documenting damage for your insurance claim.

  • Same-day emergency tarping to stop active leaks
  • Full photo and video documentation for insurance
  • Drone inspections for hard-to-reach or steep roofs
  • Direct communication with your insurance adjuster

Insurance Claim Navigation

Filing a storm damage claim can be overwhelming — adjusters, supplements, depreciation, deductibles. Frame Roofing Utah handles the entire process for you. We meet your adjuster on the roof, ensure every damaged component is documented, and submit supplements when the initial estimate falls short.

Our goal is simple: get your roof fully restored with competitive, transparent cost beyond your deductible. We have helped hundreds of Utah homeowners navigate claims after major storm events, and we know exactly what it takes to get claims approved and paid in full.
