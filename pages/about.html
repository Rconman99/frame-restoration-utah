Landon Yokers
Owner & Founder

Built on Integrity. Driven by Craftsmanship.

Landon Yokers founded Frame Roofing Utah with a simple belief: homeowners deserve a roofing contractor who treats every project like it's his own home. That means premium materials, meticulous installation, and honest communication from the first call to the final walkthrough.

Based in Heber City and serving the entire Wasatch Front, Landon and his crew have earned a reputation for showing up on time, doing the work right, and standing behind every roof they install. Whether it's a full residential reroof in Sandy, storm damage restoration in Salt Lake City, or a commercial project in Lehi — the standard is the same.

Frame Roofing is fully licensed and insured in the state of Utah. Every project comes with a 10-year workmanship warranty, and Landon personally oversees quality on every job site. When you call Frame Roofing, you're not getting a call center — you're getting the owner.

What Sets Frame Roofing Apart

Owner On Every Job

Landon personally oversees every project. No middlemen, no miscommunication — direct accountability from start to finish. When you hire Frame Roofing, the owner is on site.

Premium Materials Only

We install top-tier architectural shingles, standing seam metal, and designer options. No builder-grade shortcuts. Every material is selected for Utah's extreme climate — hail, UV, snow load, and freeze-thaw cycles.

Insurance Claim Experts

Navigating a roof insurance claim shouldn't be a headache. We handle the entire process — damage documentation, adjuster coordination, supplement filing, and installation — so you can focus on your family.

A Frictionless Experience

From the free inspection to the final walkthrough, our process is designed to be easy and stress-free. Transparent pricing, clear timelines, daily updates, and a clean job site every single day.

Local Utah Crew

No out-of-state storm chasers. Our crew lives and works along the Wasatch Front. We know the building codes, HOA requirements, and climate demands of every community we serve.

10-Year Workmanship Warranty

We stand behind every roof we install. Our 10-year workmanship warranty means if anything goes wrong due to installation, we'll be back to make it right — no questions asked.
