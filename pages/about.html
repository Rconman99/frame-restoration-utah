Meet The Owner

Built on integrity.
Driven by craftsmanship.

Landon Yokers founded Frame Roofing Utah with a simple belief: homeowners deserve a roofing contractor who treats every project like it's his own home. That means premium materials, meticulous installation, and honest communication — from the first call to the final walkthrough.

Based in Heber City and serving the entire Wasatch Front, Landon and his crew have earned a reputation for showing up on time, doing the work right, and standing behind every roof they install. Whether it's a full residential reroof in Sandy, storm damage restoration in Salt Lake City, or a commercial project in Lehi — the standard is the same.

Frame Roofing is fully licensed and insured in the state of Utah. Every project comes with a 10-year workmanship warranty, and Landon personally oversees quality on every job site. When you call Frame Roofing, you're not getting a call center — you're getting the owner.
