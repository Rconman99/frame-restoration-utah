Kitchen & Bathroom Remodels

Outdated kitchens and bathrooms are the most common renovation projects across Utah homes. Frame Roofing Utah handles full gut remodels and targeted upgrades — cabinets, countertops, tile, plumbing, and electrical — with licensed crews and transparent pricing.

Home Additions & Build-Outs

Need more space? Frame Roofing Utah builds room additions, basement finishes, and garage conversions that match your existing home's style and meet all Utah building codes. We manage permits, structural engineering, and inspections from start to finish.
