Premium Residential Reroofing

A Frictionless Experience — Easy, Fast, High Quality

Your home deserves more than a quick patch job. Frame Roofing Utah delivers streamlined construction from start to finish — so you can focus on what matters while we handle every detail. From material selection to final walkthrough, our process is designed to be easy, fast, and stress-free. We schedule around your life, keep the job site clean daily, and communicate proactively so you always know exactly where your project stands.

Beautiful Craftsmanship & Luxury Finishes

A new roof is not just protection — it is curb appeal and value added to your home. We install premium architectural shingles, standing seam metal, and Presidential and Shake style shingles with meticulous attention to detail. Every ridge line is crisp. Every flashing is sealed with precision. The result is a roof that looks as good as it performs.

Our material options include CertainTeed Landmark Pro, Owens Corning Duration, and GAF Timberline HDZ architectural shingles — all backed by lifetime manufacturer warranties. For homeowners seeking a distinctive look, we also install cedar shake profiles, designer slate-look shingles, and standing seam metal in a range of colors and finishes that complement Utah’s mountain architecture.

Uncompromising Standards — No Corners Cut

Industrial-grade quality on every project. Our local Utah crew follows exacting specifications on every installation — from proper ice and water shield application in mountain communities to ventilation systems engineered for Utah's extreme temperature swings. Every roof includes synthetic underlayment, drip edge at all eaves and rakes, and starter strip along the full perimeter. Backed by a 10‑year workmanship warranty plus lifetime manufacturer coverage.

We are licensed under Utah DOPL License #14256097-5501 and are a Lien Recovery Fund member — which means your home has an additional layer of financial protection that most roofing contractors cannot offer.

Full-Service Roof Replacement

When your roof reaches the end of its life, we make the replacement process seamless. Material selection, HOA approval, permits, and installation — handled start to finish. We work with your insurance company when applicable and provide transparent, upfront estimates so there are no surprises. Most residential reroofs in Utah are completed in a single day, with full cleanup and magnetic nail sweeps before we leave your property.

Responsive Repair & Storm Damage

Not every issue requires a full replacement. Our crews handle leak repairs, missing shingles, flashing fixes, and emergency patches across the entire Wasatch Front. When Utah hailstorms strike, we respond fast with free damage assessments and full insurance claim navigation. We document every inch of damage with photos and measurements, then work directly with your adjuster to make sure your claim covers what it should.
