A Frictionless Experience — Easy, Fast, High Quality

Your home deserves more than a quick patch job. Frame Restoration delivers streamlined construction from start to finish — so you can focus on what matters while we handle every detail. From material selection to final walkthrough, our process is designed to be easy, fast, and stress-free.

Beautiful Craftsmanship & Luxury Finishes

A new roof isn't just protection — it's curb appeal and value added to your home. We install premium architectural shingles, standing seam metal, and Presidential and Shake style shingles with meticulous attention to detail. Every ridge line is crisp. Every flashing is sealed with precision. The result is a roof that looks as good as it performs.

Uncompromising Standards — No Corners Cut

Industrial-grade quality on every project. Our local Utah crew follows exacting specifications on every installation — from proper ice and water shield application in mountain communities to ventilation systems engineered for Utah's extreme temperature swings. Backed by a 10‑year workmanship warranty.

Full-Service Roof Replacement

When your roof reaches the end of its life, we make the replacement process seamless. Material selection, HOA approval, permits, and installation — handled start to finish. We work with your insurance company when applicable and provide transparent, upfront estimates so there are no surprises.

Responsive Repair & Storm Damage

Not every issue requires a full replacement. Our crews handle leak repairs, missing shingles, flashing fixes, and emergency patches across the entire Wasatch Front. When Utah hailstorms strike, we respond fast with free damage assessments and full insurance claim navigation.
