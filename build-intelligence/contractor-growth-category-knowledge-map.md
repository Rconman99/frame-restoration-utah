## Lead Capture And Conversion

Priority: P0

Knowledge score: 9.8/10

Business value:

Turns traffic into estimate requests, calls, and booked-job opportunities.

Why add it:

Most contractor sites leak demand after the user arrives. This category forces every page to answer what service is offered, where it is offered, why the contractor is credible, and how to contact them immediately.

Evidence summary:

Google Ads distinguishes lead-form volume from higher-intent lead quality. GA4 can measure lead-generation form submissions as key events, so conversion should be designed and tracked as a first-class product surface.

Product modules:

- mobile CTA system
- estimate form builder
- call path QA
- lead quality prompts
- thank-you page and offline conversion handoff

Agents or skills:

- conversion strategist
- form friction auditor
- mobile CTA QA agent
- lead quality classifier

Automations:

- detect pages without a primary CTA
- test tel links and form endpoints
- score form friction
- route high-intent services to priority follow-up

Data signals:

- cta_click
- phone_click
- form_start
- form_submit
- generate_lead
- service_requested
- city_requested
- lead_quality

Owner gates:

- privacy policy approval
- form fields approval
- claim wording approval
- tracking script approval

Compliance risks:

- collecting personal information without disclosure
- overpromising emergency response
- asking for insurance or damage claims beyond approved intake

Success metrics:

- form submit rate
- phone click rate
- qualified lead rate
- time to first response
- booked job rate

MVP actions:

- Add page-level CTA inventory.
- Create standard roofing estimate form fields.
- Add click-to-call QA.
- Mark form submit as generate_lead/key event.

Quality tests:

- CTA exists on mobile and desktop.
- Form works with required fields only.
- Lead event fires once per submit.
- Thank-you path preserves source/page/city/service.
- Privacy policy is linked before any paid lead form.
