## Operating Principles

- No research without a source and artifact target.
- No broad audit without a candidate registry, score vector, decision, and filter reason for rejected ideas.
- No evidence overwrite: preserve source/date/confidence at decision time and mark stale records explicitly.
- No final top action list without bias and diversity checks.
- No new durable artifact before semantic recall against prior docs, templates, skills, handoffs, validators, and MCP resources.
- No category without business value, owner gates, risks, metrics, and tests.
- No fix without the validation delta it is meant to close.
- No implementation promotion without re-audit proof.
- No public contractor claim, review, credential, service area, outreach, ad, GBP change, tracking script, or lead-data workflow without approval gates.
- No speed metric without a paired stability metric.
- No repeated manual blocker without turning it into a customer request template or automation.
- No recurring research win without promotion into a template, skill, validator, MCP resource, AGENTS.md rule, or dashboard metric.
