## REVIEW GENERATION STRATEGY

**Current standing:** 5.0 stars, 19 reviews on Google (strong but need volume)

**Target:** 30+ Google reviews by end of Q2 2026

**Tactics:**
1. **Post-job QR code card** — hand to every customer, links to GBP review page
2. **Follow-up SMS** — send 3-5 days after job completion via Twilio (pending 10DLC approval ~April 21-28)
3. **Email follow-up** — automated via handle-lead pipeline after job marked complete
4. **Yelp & BBB** — ask satisfied customers to leave reviews on these as secondary platforms
5. **Nextdoor recommendations** — encourage neighbors to recommend on Nextdoor
6. **Photo reviews** — ask customers to include before/after photos (boosts review visibility)

**Review response protocol:**
- Respond to ALL reviews within 24 hours
- Positive: Thank by name, mention specific work done
- Negative: Acknowledge, apologize, offer to resolve offline
- Never incentivize reviews with discounts (violates Google TOS)
