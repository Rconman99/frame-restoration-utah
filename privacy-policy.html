Information We Collect

When you submit a quote request, contact form, or reach out via phone call or text message, we collect your name, phone number, email address, and details about your project. We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry and provide the roofing and restoration services you requested.

How We Use Your Information

Your contact information is used to:

  • Respond to your service inquiry or quote request
  • Schedule and confirm appointments
  • Send project status updates via text message or email
  • Follow up after service completion

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

SMS/Text Messaging

By submitting your phone number through our website contact form, free estimate request form, or by texting our business number directly, you consent to receive text messages from Frame Roofing Utah related to your inquiry. Message types include appointment confirmations, project updates, and customer care communications.

Message frequency varies based on your project needs. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any text message. For help, reply HELP or call us at 435-292-8802.

We do not share your phone number or SMS data with third parties for their marketing purposes. Your phone number and any information collected via text message will only be used to provide the services you requested.
