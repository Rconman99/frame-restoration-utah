Orem — known as "Family City USA" — is Utah County's second-largest city, with a housing stock as diverse as its population. Frame Roofing Utah installs impact-resistant systems designed for this weather pattern. When is hail season in Orem, Utah? +
Orem typically sees its most active hail and storm season from late spring through summer. Storms often develop over the Wasatch Range and move across Utah County rapidly. After any significant storm, Frame Roofing Utah recommends a free inspection — hail damage that looks minor from the ground can significantly shorten your roof's lifespan if left unaddressed.
