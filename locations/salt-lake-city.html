Roofing FAQs for Salt Lake City Residents

Do you offer free roof inspections in Salt Lake City? +
Yes — Frame Roofing Utah provides 100% free, no-obligation roof inspections throughout Salt Lake City and Salt Lake County. There is no cost and no pressure. Call 435-302-4422 or schedule online and a licensed Utah roofing specialist will come to you, typically within 24-48 hours.
Does Frame Roofing Utah handle insurance claims for Salt Lake City homeowners? +
Yes. We specialize in storm damage insurance claims throughout Salt Lake County including Salt Lake City. We document all hail, wind, and storm damage, coordinate directly with your adjuster from State Farm, Allstate, and Farmers, and help ensure you receive the full payout your policy allows. We've helped homeowners across the Wasatch Front recover full claim value when initial adjuster assessments underestimated the damage.
When is hail season in Salt Lake City, Utah? +
Salt Lake City typically sees its most active hail and storm season from May through September. Storms often develop over the Wasatch Range and move across Salt Lake County rapidly. After any significant storm, Frame Roofing Utah recommends a free inspection — hail damage that looks minor from the ground can significantly shorten your roof's lifespan if left unaddressed.
How much does a roof replacement cost in Salt Lake City? +
Roof replacement costs in Salt Lake City typically range from $8,000 to $22,000 for a standard residential home, depending on square footage, pitch, material choice, and current damage. Frame Roofing Utah provides free written estimates with no obligation. If your roof was damaged by hail or wind, insurance may cover all or most of the replacement cost — we handle the claim process at no extra charge.
Is Frame Roofing Utah licensed and insured in Salt Lake City, Utah? +
Yes. Frame Roofing Utah is fully licensed and insured to perform roofing and restoration work throughout Utah, including Salt Lake City and all of Salt Lake County. We hold an A+ rating with the BBB and NRCA membership earned since 2014. We carry full general liability insurance and workers' compensation on every job.
