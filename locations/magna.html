Premium Roof Replacements in Magna

Magna sits on the western edge of the Salt Lake Valley near the Kennecott copper mine and the Great Salt Lake — a position that exposes homes to some of the strongest winds in the metro area. The wind patterns that blow off the lake hit Magna's neighborhoods along Main Street and 8000 West with sustained force that strips granules from shingles, lifts tabs, and degrades flashing seals years faster than homes further east. Most of Magna's housing stock dates from the 1940s through 1960s, and many of these older roofs have already exceeded their expected lifespan.

What many Magna homeowners do not realize is that their storm-damaged roof likely qualifies for a full insurance-covered replacement. After spring and summer hailstorms, older shingles sustain damage that meets insurance thresholds for complete replacement — not just patching. Frame Restoration provides free inspections after every major storm, documents the damage to insurance-company standards, and handles the entire claim from start to finish. For many Magna families, the only out-of-pocket cost is the deductible.

  • Full tear-off required on most Magna homes — many older roofs have multiple shingle layers that must be removed to inspect and repair decking

  • Affordable upgrade paths from original 3-tab shingles to modern architectural systems that dramatically improve wind resistance and curb appeal
