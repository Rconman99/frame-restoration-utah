When is hail season in South Jordan, Utah? +
South Jordan typically sees its most active hail and storm season from May through August. Storms often develop over the Wasatch Range and move across Salt Lake County rapidly. After any significant storm, Frame Roofing Utah recommends a free inspection — hail damage that looks minor from the ground can significantly shorten your roof's lifespan if left unaddressed.
How much does a roof replacement cost in South Jordan? +
