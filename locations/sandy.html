Roofing FAQs for Sandy Residents

Does Frame Roofing Utah handle insurance claims for Sandy homeowners? +
Yes. We specialize in storm damage insurance claims throughout Salt Lake County including Sandy. We document all hail, wind, and storm damage, coordinate directly with your adjuster from most major Utah carriers, and help ensure you receive the full payout your policy allows. We've helped homeowners across the Wasatch Front recover full claim value when initial adjuster assessments underestimated the damage.
When is hail season in Sandy, Utah? +
Sandy typically sees its most active hail and storm season from May through August. Storms often develop over the Wasatch Range and move across Salt Lake County rapidly. After any significant storm, Frame Roofing Utah recommends a free inspection — hail damage that looks minor from the ground can significantly shorten your roof's lifespan if left unaddressed.
How much does a roof replacement cost in Sandy? +
Roof replacement costs in Sandy typically range from $8,000 to $22,000 for a standard residential home, depending on square footage, pitch, material choice, and current damage. Frame Roofing Utah provides free written estimates with no obligation. If your roof was damaged by hail or wind, insurance may cover all or most of the replacement cost — we handle the claim process at no extra charge.
Is Frame Roofing Utah licensed and insured in Sandy, Utah? +
Yes. Frame Roofing Utah is fully licensed and insured to perform roofing and restoration work throughout Utah, including Sandy and all of Salt Lake County. We hold an A+ rating with the BBB. We carry full general liability insurance and workers' compensation on every job.
