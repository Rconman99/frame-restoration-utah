Common Storm Damage Problems in West Jordan

  • Wasatch Front hail belt location — West Jordan sits directly in the path of storms that build over the Oquirrh Mountains. Hail events hit the city multiple times per year, bruising shingles and causing granule loss that leads to premature failure.
  • Oquirrh Mountain wind channeling — Wind gusts funnel through the gaps between the Oquirrh and Traverse Mountains, hitting West Jordan neighborhoods with sustained high winds that lift, crack, and blow off shingles.
  • Ice dams from rapid temperature swings — West Jordan can swing 30+ degrees in a single day during winter. This freeze-thaw cycling creates ice dams along eaves that back water under shingles and into attic spaces.
  • Mass subdivision damage events — When a major hailstorm hits, entire West Jordan subdivisions sustain damage simultaneously. Homeowners who wait too long to address damage risk compounding problems — small issues become costly failures.
  • Delayed damage discovery in newer homes — Many West Jordan homes built in the 2000s have builder-grade shingles that show storm damage subtly. Without a professional inspection, homeowners may not realize they need repairs until leaks appear months later.

How fast can you respond to storm damage in West Jordan?

We offer 24/7 emergency response across West Jordan. After a major storm, we can typically have a crew on-site within hours to tarp exposed areas, prevent further water intrusion, and begin planning your full restoration.

How do I know if my West Jordan roof has hail damage?

Hail damage isn't always visible from the ground. Signs include dented gutters, dings on AC units or vents, and granule loss on shingles (check your gutters for granule buildup). The most reliable method is a professional roof inspection — we offer them free of charge.

Do you upgrade the roof system during storm restoration?

Absolutely. Storm restoration is an opportunity to upgrade. We can install impact-resistant shingles, improved underlayment, better ventilation, and enhanced flashing — leaving your West Jordan home better protected than before the storm.

Do you provide emergency tarping for storm-damaged roofs in West Jordan?

Yes. Emergency tarping is one of the first things we do after a major storm. We secure exposed areas to prevent further water damage to your home's interior, then schedule the permanent repair or replacement.
