Common Commercial Roofing Problems in West Jordan

  • Flat roof leaks at Jordan Landing retail — The large flat roofs on Jordan Landing's retail buildings are prone to membrane punctures, ponding, and seam failures that allow water intrusion into occupied tenant spaces.
  • TPO seam failures — Heat-welded TPO seams on West Jordan commercial buildings can fail prematurely if the original installation used incorrect temperatures or insufficient overlap, especially under thermal cycling.
  • Ponding on warehouse roofs near I-15 — Large warehouse roofs along the I-15 corridor often have low spots where water pools after rain or snowmelt. Prolonged ponding accelerates membrane breakdown and leads to leaks.
  • Aging strip malls on Redwood Road and 7800 South — Many of West Jordan's older strip malls along these corridors have original roofing systems that are 20+ years old and past their effective service life.
  • HVAC penetration damage — Rooftop HVAC units on commercial buildings create penetration points where flashing and sealant degrade over time, especially around older units that have been serviced or replaced.
