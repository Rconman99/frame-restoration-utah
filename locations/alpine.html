When is hail season in Alpine, Utah? +
Alpine typically sees its most active hail and storm season from May through August. Storms often develop over the Wasatch Range and move across Utah County rapidly. After any significant storm, Frame Restoration recommends a free inspection — hail damage that looks minor from the ground can significantly shorten your roof's lifespan if left unaddressed.
How much does a roof replacement cost in Alpine? +
Roof replacement costs in Alpine typically range from $8,000 to $22,000 for a standard residential home, depending on square footage, pitch, material choice, and current damage.
