Insurance Claims: How Kearns Homeowners Get New Roofs Covered

Kearns' older housing stock is more vulnerable to storm damage than newer construction — and that actually works in homeowners' favor when it comes to insurance claims. Frame Restoration helps budget-conscious Kearns families navigate the process:

  • Free post-storm roof inspections — we check for hail dents, wind-lifted shingles, and granule loss that is invisible from the ground
  • Detailed damage documentation with photos and measurements that meet insurance company requirements
  • Direct coordination with your adjuster — we meet them on-site and walk the roof together to ensure nothing is missed
  • Supplement filing when initial estimates undervalue the damage — common on older Kearns roofs with multiple layers or obsolete materials
  • Premium replacement materials that upgrade your home's value and curb appeal over the original builder-grade installation
  • Full deck inspection during tear-off — 60-70 year old homes often have hidden decking damage that must be addressed for a lasting installation
