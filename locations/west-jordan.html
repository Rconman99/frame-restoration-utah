When is hail season in West Jordan, Utah? +
West Jordan typically sees its most active hail and storm season from late spring through summer. Storms often develop over the Wasatch Range and move across Salt Lake County rapidly. After any significant storm, Frame Restoration recommends a free inspection — hail damage that looks minor from the ground can significantly shorten your roof's lifespan if left unaddressed.
