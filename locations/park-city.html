When is hail season in Park City, Utah? +
Park City typically sees its most active hail and storm season from summer months. Storms often develop over the Wasatch Range and move across Summit County rapidly. After any significant storm, Frame Roofing Utah recommends a free inspection — hail damage that looks minor from the ground can significantly shorten your roof's lifespan if left unaddressed.
How much does a roof replacement cost in Park City? +
Roof replacement costs in Park City typically range from $8,000 to $22,000 for a standard residential home, depending on square footage, pitch, material choice, and current damage. Frame Roofing Utah provides free written estimates with no obligation. If your roof was damaged by hail or wind, insurance may cover all or most of the replacement cost — we handle the claim process at no extra charge.
