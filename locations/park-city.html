When is hail season in Park City, Utah? +
Park City typically sees its most active hail and storm season from summer months. Storms often develop over the Wasatch Range and move across Summit County rapidly. After any significant storm, Frame Roofing Utah recommends a free inspection — hail damage that looks minor from the ground can significantly shorten your roof's lifespan if left unaddressed.
