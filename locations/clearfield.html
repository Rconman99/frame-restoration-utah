When is hail season in Clearfield, Utah? +
Clearfield typically sees its most active hail and storm season from May through August. Storms often develop over the Wasatch Range and move across Davis County rapidly. After any significant storm, Frame Roofing Utah recommends a free inspection — hail damage that looks minor from the ground can significantly shorten your roof's lifespan if left unaddressed.
