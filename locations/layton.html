When is hail season in Layton, Utah? +
Layton typically sees its most active hail and storm season from late spring through summer. Storms often develop over the Wasatch Range and move across Davis County rapidly. After any significant storm, Frame Restoration recommends a free inspection — hail damage that looks minor from the ground can significantly shorten your roof's lifespan if left unaddressed.
How much does a roof replacement cost in Layton? +
Roof replacement costs in Layton typically range from $8,000 to $22,000 for a standard residential home, depending on square footage, pitch, material choice, and current damage. Frame Restoration provides free written estimates with no obligation. If your roof was damaged by hail or wind, insurance may cover all or most of the replacement cost — we handle the claim process at no extra charge.
