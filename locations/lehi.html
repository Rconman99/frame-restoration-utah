Premium Roof Replacements in Lehi

Lehi has exploded from a quiet farming town to a 75,000-person tech hub at the heart of Silicon Slopes — and its roofing stock reflects that transformation. Frame Roofing Utah uses high-wind-rated materials and enhanced nailing patterns to combat this exposure. Every project includes our 10-year workmanship warranty and lifetime manufacturer warranties on qualifying materials.

When is hail season in Lehi, Utah? +
Lehi typically sees its most active hail and storm season from late spring through summer. Storms often develop over the Wasatch Range and move across Utah County rapidly. After any significant storm, Frame Roofing Utah recommends a free inspection — hail damage that looks minor from the ground can significantly shorten your roof's lifespan if left unaddressed.
