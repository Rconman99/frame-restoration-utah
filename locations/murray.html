Premium Roof Replacements in Murray

Murray is one of Salt Lake County's most established cities — a central hub anchored by Intermountain Medical Center and the Fashion Place Mall area. The residential neighborhoods surrounding Murray Park, along Vine Street, and throughout the 4800 South to 5900 South corridors were primarily built in the 1950s through 1980s. Many of these homes are now 40 to 50 years old with original or once-replaced roofs that have reached — or exceeded — the end of their functional lifespan. Shingles from this era have endured decades of Utah freeze-thaw cycles, summer UV exposure, and storm impacts that have left them brittle, cracked, and vulnerable to leaks.

Murray is also seeing an increase in multifamily and mixed-use development, adding townhomes and condos to a city historically dominated by single-family homes. Frame Restoration serves both markets — from traditional residential tear-offs on Murray's established streets to HOA-managed multifamily projects that require coordination across multiple buildings and stakeholders. Every project includes our 10-year workmanship warranty and lifetime manufacturer warranties.

40-50 Year Old Roofs: Murray's Most Common Reroofing Challenge

The majority of Murray's single-family homes were built in an era before modern high-performance shingles existed. Frame Restoration upgrades these aging systems to current standards:

  • Complete tear-off of original or second-generation shingles with thorough deck inspection for hidden moisture damage and rot
  • Modern architectural shingles in styles that complement Murray's mid-century ranch, split-level, and colonial home designs
  • Ventilation system upgrades — many 1950s-1980s Murray homes have inadequate soffit and ridge ventilation that shortens roof life
  • Ice and water shield at all eaves, valleys, and penetrations to prevent leak paths that older felt-paper underlayment cannot stop
  • Insurance claim documentation for storm-damaged aging roofs — Murray's older shingles are more vulnerable and often qualify for full replacement coverage
  • HOA and multifamily project coordination for Murray's growing condo and townhome communities

When is hail season in Murray, Utah? +
Murray typically sees its most active hail and storm season from May through September. Storms often develop over the Wasatch Range and move across Salt Lake County rapidly. After any significant storm, Frame Restoration recommends a free inspection — hail damage that looks minor from the ground can significantly shorten your roof's lifespan if left unaddressed.
How much does a roof replacement cost in Murray? +
Roof replacement costs in Murray typically range from $8,000 to $22,000 for a standard residential home, depending on square footage, pitch, material choice, and current damage.
