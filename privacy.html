Information We Collect

When you submit a quote request or contact form, we collect your name, phone number, email address, and details about your project. We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry.

How We Use Your Information

Your contact information is used only to follow up on your service request. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

SMS/Text Messaging

Opt-in is optional. SMS/text messaging is a separate, optional service. When you submit a contact form or free roof inspection request on this website, you may opt in to receive SMS/text messages from Frame Roofing Utah by checking the "Yes, I agree to receive SMS/text messages" checkbox on the form. Consent to receive SMS messages is not required to submit the form, request a quote, or receive our services. If you do not check the SMS opt-in box, we will contact you only by phone and/or email. If you do opt in, you agree to receive SMS/text messages from Frame Roofing Utah at the phone number you provide. Messages may include appointment confirmations, project updates, follow-ups after service, and responses to your inquiries.

Message frequency: Message frequency varies based on your interaction with us. You may receive up to 5 messages per month related to your inquiry or active project.

Opt-out: You may opt out of receiving text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message and no further texts will be sent.

Help: For help with our messaging program, reply HELP to any message or contact us at info@framerestorationutah.com or 435-302-4422.

Carrier charges: Message and data rates may apply. Check with your mobile carrier for details about your text messaging plan.

No mobile information sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number or any information collected through our SMS program with third parties for their promotional or marketing purposes. Your consent to receive text messages is not a condition of purchasing any goods or services from us.

Supported carriers: Service is available on most major US carriers including AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, and others. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
