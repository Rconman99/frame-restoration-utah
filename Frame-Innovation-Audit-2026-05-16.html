The Diagnostic

The infrastructure is enterprise-grade. The throughput is anemic.
11 deployed edge functions. 9 database tables. 13 scheduled agents running across iMessage, Gmail, Reddit, GBP, GSC, SEO. 130 instrumented pages with full UTM/gclid attribution. 254 Reddit signals scanned in the last 6 weeks.

And: 6 total leads, 2 of those won, 0 with margin filled in, 0 phone clicks tracked, 0 Reddit signals actioned.

The build phase is finished. The build was the easy part. The hard part — getting volume through the pipes Landon and Ryan already own — is the actual remaining work. Most of the moves below are not "build more"; they are "make what exists actually fire."

The math problem: If your lifetime won-deal margin sits at $0 in the DB, your commission engine returns $0 every time. The 12 Gaps

Categorized: BLEEDING = losing money/leads today · TRAP = latent risk · UNLOCK = upside that just got cheaper with new Claude features

1. Margin column is null on every won deal
Bleeding
The DB rule for commission is margin × 0.05 (Heber/Midway) or margin × 0.10 elsewhere. But margin requires Landon to manually type the dollar number after every job. He doesn't. Evidence: 2 won deals, 0 with margin filled. Greg's $118K shows $0.00 commission.
Fix: Nightly QuickBooks pull (new Cowork integration) reads Landon's costs against job_value, computes margin, writes to DB. Self-calculating commission. Estimated unblock: ~$1.5K–$2K/job properly attributed to Ryan.
2. Phone clicks at zero after 4 days live
Trap
The tracker we shipped 2026-05-11 should be firing on every tel:/sms: click site-wide. Evidence: phone_clicks table = 0 rows. Either the beacon is failing silently (CORS, sendBeacon blocked), or there genuinely was no tel:/sms: click on the site in 4 days. Both are bad and both need a fix.
Fix: Console-test from your phone right now (tap the GBP number, then refresh /leads). If still 0, the bug is in the beacon path. If non-zero, the problem is traffic, not tracking.
3. Inbound calls don't create lead records
Bleeding
8 calls logged in call_logs, 6 leads in leads. Someone called the Twilio tracking number 8 times, and zero of those calls became a contact card Ryan can follow up on. The handle-call edge fn forwards to Landon and logs the call — that's it. Evidence: call_logs=8, leads=6, sms_logs=5 — none of the SMS or calls become a lead unless they also fill out the form.
Fix: handle-call + handle-sms write a "lead" row on first inbound from a new number (status=new, source_page=inbound-call or inbound-sms). One commit, big surface-area gain.
4. 254 Reddit signals, 0 actioned
Bleeding
37 of those signals scored 50+ on engagement (high-value posts). None were ever responded to, DMed, or converted. The scanner detects pain. Nothing actions it. Evidence: reddit_signals WHERE actioned=true = 0. The agent collects, it doesn't respond.
Fix: An "action" tier in the agent: top 5 weekly signals get a drafted DM (with Landon brand-voice guardrails) queued for Ryan's approval each Monday. Even 1 conversion/month = $5–25K.
5. Email captured on 1 of 6 leads
Bleeding
Form has the field; people skip it. The CRM has no second-channel reach for 5 of 6 leads — if a customer goes cold on SMS, you're stuck. Evidence: leads WHERE email IS NOT NULL = 1.
Fix: Make email required on the form OR add a follow-up "We'll text you the estimate — what email should we cc?" auto-SMS from handle-lead immediately after submit.
6. Greg's contract data lives in three places
Trap
CRM row, photo of contract in iMessage cache, photo of deposit check separately. No customer file. When the install happens 4 weeks from now and someone asks "did Greg pick Brava Aged Cedar or Designer Shake?", the answer is "scroll through Ryan's messages." Evidence: project_frame_lead_pipeline.md memory + Greg's case manually reconstructed from a photo this session.
Fix: DocuSign integration (new Cowork capability) replaces paper contract. Signed PDF auto-attaches to the lead row. End of paper.
7. No payment/job status tracking past "won"
Trap
Once a lead hits status=won, the CRM is silent. No deposit_received_at, no install_scheduled_for, no balance_due, no warranty_doc_url, no job_completed_at workflow. The review-request agent fires 3 days after job_completed_at — and that field is set manually, basically never. Evidence: review-request-trigger runs daily but no rows have job_completed_at set, so no review requests actually go out.
Fix: Post-won workflow columns + a simple "Job status" sub-panel on /leads. Once Landon marks "install complete" → review request fires automatically 3 days later.
8. Landon agent reads instructions but doesn't surface action
Unlock
The landon-message-agent runs every 15min and reacts to what Landon says. But the inverse — agent reading the CRM and proactively texting Landon "Greg's install is in 3 days, did you order materials?" — doesn't exist. Evidence: landon-message-agent SKILL.md = reactive only; no proactive CRM-monitoring agent.
Fix: New "landon-cadence" agent — daily 7am check of all open won/scheduled jobs, texts Landon a punch list of what needs to happen today.
9. AEO content shipped, AEO citation tracking missing
Unlock
PR #56/#57 dropped a 13-spoke storm-chaser pillar with HowTo schema, llms.txt Q&A, standalone Service nodes. Massive AEO investment. Zero mechanism to know whether Perplexity, ChatGPT, or Google AI Overview actually cite frameroofingutah.com when someone searches "best Utah roofer." Evidence: no aeo_citation_tracker in scheduled tasks; brand-radar tools available in the deferred MCP list but unused.
Fix: Weekly task that queries Perplexity + ChatGPT API for 20 Utah roofing prompts and records whether frameroofingutah.com is cited. Brand-radar Ahrefs tool already available in your MCP. Measure the AEO investment.
10. Skills aren't portable yet — the agent stack is locked in your Mac
Unlock
13 sophisticated scheduled tasks live under ~/Documents/Claude/Scheduled/. With Agent Skills now an open standard portable across Claude.ai/Code/SDK, this entire stack could be packaged as "Frame Stack" Skills — sellable IP for the client-website-builder side of your business. Evidence: project_client_builder.md memory mentions the productized service; the agent stack isn't structured for portability.
Fix: Convert top 5 agents to Skill format. Bundle as a marketplace plugin. Every client website you build deploys the stack on day 1.
11. /leads CRM has no mobile-first design
Trap
Ryan runs his life from his phone. The dashboard is desktop-styled with a side-slide panel that doesn't behave great on iPhone. Evidence: leads.html @media (max-width:700px) rule is minimal; the panel goes full-width but the toolbar wraps weirdly.
Fix: Mobile-first redo or a PWA wrapper with push notifications. Opus 4.7 visual pass when you're ready (the prior agent flagged this — agreed but lower priority than the bleeding gaps above).
12. No commission ledger — just an instantaneous calculation
Trap
Each lead has a commission column that recomputes on the fly. There's no "Ryan earned $X this month, settled $Y, outstanding $Z." When you ask Landon for your check, you'll be summing rows manually. Evidence: no commission_payments or commission_ledger table; no settle/paid status.
Fix: commission_ledger table + monthly statement view at /leads. Each month auto-locks the prior month's earned commission as an invoice to Landon.

The Sequenced Stack — One Session Each

Inbound-call & SMS → auto-lead

Edit handle-call + handle-sms to insert a leads row on first contact from a new number. Eight calls/five SMS already in your DB that should have been leads. 1 hour. Highest ROI move.
Two edge function edits, one Supabase deploy each.

Click-tracker smoke + fix

Either prove the beacon works (you click, row appears) or find the bug. Until phone_clicks gets a real row, the data Ryan's making decisions from is fiction.
15 minutes from your phone + browser DevTools network tab.

Post-won workflow columns + review-request unblock

Add deposit_received_at, install_scheduled_for, job_completed_at to /leads panel. Suddenly review-request agent (already deployed!) actually fires. Reviews compound; you go from 20 → 50 → 100 over 6 months.
DB migration + 60 lines in leads.html.

QuickBooks → margin nightly pull

Closes the commission engine loop. Nightly cron reads QB cost vs job_value on won deals, computes margin, writes to leads.margin. Ryan's commission becomes real.
New scheduled task + QB OAuth (one-time). 2–3 hours including testing.

DocuSign integration for new contracts

Replace Landon's paper proposal pad. New won deal → DocuSign template auto-fills from /leads → customer signs → PDF auto-attaches to lead row → status flips to won. Greg's whole experience eliminated as a manual workflow.
DocuSign template setup + webhook → handle-docusign-signed edge fn. Half-day.

Reddit action-tier — drafted DM queue

For top 5 weekly signals scored >50, agent drafts a casual Landon-voice DM and queues to Ryan's iMessage for approve/send. Don't auto-spam Reddit; pre-cook the reply. One conversion/quarter = ROI.
New scheduled task + Landon brand-voice skill (already exists in marketing plugin).

Proactive landon-cadence agent

Daily 7am: scan open won/scheduled jobs, text Landon today's punch list ("Greg install Tuesday, material order due by Sunday"). Inverts the agent from reactive → proactive.
Mirror landon-message-agent skill structure with CRM SELECT instead of iMessage read.

AEO citation tracker

Measure whether the storm-chaser pillar investment is actually getting cited by Perplexity/ChatGPT/Google AI. Brand-radar tool already in your MCP arsenal — just wire it to a weekly run.
~1 hour: 20 prompts, weekly cron, slack/SMS digest.

The Strategic Re-Frame

You've quietly built the infrastructure for a productized back-office service for residential contractors. The website is the demo. The agent stack is the product.

Today Landon pays you implicitly via the 10% commission deal. That deal is priced for "Ryan runs marketing." With QB + DocuSign + Reddit + GBP + AEO + review automation all firing as one orchestrated platform, what Landon is actually getting is closer to "outsourced revenue operations" — and the next contractor (Connor in Dallas, the future Texas market) will need the same thing.

The new Claude small-business integrations aren't just Frame Roofing utilities. They're the building blocks of "Frame Stack" — the thing you sell to the next 10 contractors.

That's not a today decision. But every gap you fix above is also a feature of that future product. Build the bleeding gaps first because Landon needs them. Build them well because the next contractor will pay for them.
