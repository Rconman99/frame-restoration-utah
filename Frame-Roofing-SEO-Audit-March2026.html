Executive Summary

Critical Finding: frameroofingutah.com is essentially invisible to Google. Despite having 143 pages in the sitemap, only ~3 are indexed, and the site does not appear in the top 10 results for ANY of the core target keywords tested. Searching "Frame Roofing Utah" returns only 1 indexed page (Springville location). The site has strong on-page foundations (schema markup, unique location content, solid content depth) but is being held back by zero backlinks, no domain authority, and recent domain age.
Biggest Strength: The site's content quality is significantly better than most local roofing competitors. 143 pages of location-specific, FAQ-rich content with proper schema markup puts Frame Roofing in a strong position once indexing and authority issues are resolved. The 5.0-star GBP rating with 19 reviews is also a strong trust signal.

Top 3 priorities that will have the most impact:

  1. Fix indexing: Add canonical tags, fix phone number inconsistency in schema, submit individual URLs via GSC, build 10+ quality backlinks
  2. Complete directory submissions: 35 of 38 directories still pending — this is the fastest path to backlinks and NAP consistency
  3. Set up the 301 redirect: framerestorations.com → frameroofingutah.com (blocked on GoDaddy access)
