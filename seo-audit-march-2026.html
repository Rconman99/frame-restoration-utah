1. Executive Summary

Frame Roofing Utah has a strong local SEO foundation with excellent schema markup, 45 city location pages, 24 blog posts, and 12 service pages. The site architecture is well-organized and ahead of most Utah roofing competitors in terms of local page coverage and structured data. However, there are critical indexation issues actively blocking key pages from Google, plus several high-impact opportunities being left on the table.

Biggest strength: Comprehensive location strategy covering 45 Utah cities — more than any competitor audited (IWC ~15, USA Superior ~10). Plus excellent structured data with 7+ schema types.

Top 3 priorities:

1. CRITICAL Two money pages (Storm Damage + General Contracting) have noindex, nofollow tags — Google literally cannot see them.

2. HIGH Missing "roof replacement cost Utah" content — competitors dominate this keyword with dedicated pricing guides and calculators.

3. HIGH Homepage has 4 H1 tags instead of 1, diluting keyword focus and confusing Google about what the page is about.

Overall assessment: Strong foundation with critical issues that need immediate fixes. The site has the bones of an SEO powerhouse but is leaving significant traffic on the table.
