## Why this matters for Map Pack ranking

Google's local algorithm uses GBP service area as a proximity-relevance signal. A search like "roofer near me" from Provo or West Valley City won't surface Frame because the GBP service area excludes those cities — even though the website ranks for "roof replacement Provo Utah" and similar.
