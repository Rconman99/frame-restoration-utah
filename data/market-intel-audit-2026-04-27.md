## Audit 1 — What's wrong with the naive 45-city allocation

If we ran `rcbuild market-intel` on Utah with $15K/45 cities, **every city would get $333/mo — below googleLSA's $500 floor.** The kit's greedy fill would either drop to a single channel everywhere or refuse most cities. Either output is wrong.

The deeper structural issue: **Utah is a tier-stratified market, not a metro-uniform one.**

- **Park City single roof** = $50K-$150K (slate / standing seam / luxury reroofs)
- **Heber City single roof** = $25K-$60K (mountain custom, drone-documented)
- **Salt Lake metro single roof** = $12K-$25K (volume Wasatch valley work)
- **Tier-4 secondary single roof** = $8K-$15K (smaller stock, lower freq)

Spreading $333 evenly ignores a 10-15x EV spread between Park City and Magna. The fix: concentrate paid spend on top 6-8 cities, let the 45-page SEO moat do passive lead capture for the rest.
