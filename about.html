Our Story

From Dallas-Fort Worth to the Wasatch Front

Frame Roofing Utah started in the Dallas-Fort Worth market, building a reputation for meticulous craftsmanship and honest business practices in one of the most competitive roofing markets in the country. When we expanded to Utah, we brought those same uncompromising standards to the Wasatch Front — premium materials, expert installations, and a commitment to doing the job right the first time.

Now headquartered at 142 S Main St, Heber City, UT 84032, Frame Roofing Utah serves more than 40 communities from Ogden to Santaquin. We hold an A+ rating with the Better Business Bureau and have been proud members of the National Roofing Contractors Association (NRCA) since 2014. Every project we take on reflects the standard we set from day one: no shortcuts, no excuses, just exceptional roofing built to endure Utah's demanding climate.

Licensed & Protected

Utah DOPL License #14256097-5501

Frame Roofing Utah is fully licensed under Utah DOPL License #14256097-5501, verifiable at secure.utah.gov/llv — search "Frame Restoration Utah LLC" under Contractor.

As a Utah Lien Recovery Fund member, your home carries an extra layer of protection that most roofing contractors cannot offer. Utah's Lien Recovery Fund protects homeowners against mechanic's liens — a risk that exists when contractors fail to pay their suppliers or subcontractors. When you hire a contractor without LRF membership, unpaid suppliers can legally file a lien against your property even if you already paid your contractor. Frame Roofing Utah's LRF membership means you are protected against this scenario. It is one of the strongest consumer protections Utah law provides, and it is only available through licensed, LRF-member contractors.
