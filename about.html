Our Story

From Dallas-Fort Worth to the Wasatch Front

Frame Restoration started in the Dallas-Fort Worth market, building a reputation for meticulous craftsmanship and honest business practices in one of the most competitive roofing markets in the country. When we expanded to Utah, we brought those same uncompromising standards to the Wasatch Front — premium materials, expert installations, and a commitment to doing the job right the first time.

Now headquartered at 142 S Main St, Heber City, UT 84032, Frame Restoration Utah serves more than 40 communities from Ogden to Santaquin. We hold an A+ rating with the Better Business Bureau and have been proud members of the National Roofing Contractors Association (NRCA) since 2014. Every project we take on reflects the standard we set from day one: no shortcuts, no excuses, just exceptional roofing built to endure Utah's demanding climate.
